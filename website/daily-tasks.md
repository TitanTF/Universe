# Daily Tasks

* **Visit Website**
  * 10 Gems 
* **Leave a Comment**
  * 5 Gems 
* **Like a Post**
  * 5 Gems 
* **Make a Post**
  * 10 - 70 Gems 
  * Day 1 grants 10 gems. The number of gems then increases by 10 per day, and resets back to 10 after Day 7. 
* **Create a Forum Thread**
  * 10 Gems 
* **Leave a Forum Comment**
  * 5 Gems 
* **React to a Thread**
  * 5 Gems
